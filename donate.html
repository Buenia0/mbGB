Donate

If you are so inclined, there are several ways to give back to the libmbGB project and its sister projects, in both monetary and non-monetary ways.

Please keep in mind that none of these are required for keeping libmbGB or its sister projects free and open, but they do help keep them all alive and are very welcome. Thank you for using libmbGB and friends!

More hardware

There are several games and accesories for various systems, including the Game Boy and Game Boy Color, that are either not emulated properly or would be emulated better with additional research. In addition, there are various platforms, including Mac OS, that neither libmbGB nor its sister projects can be formally tested on, mainly due to personal financial constraints. If you would like to sell or donate any of the following items currently in your possession, contact BueniaDev at buenia.mbemu@gmail.com:

  • Used Nintendo handheld consoles
  • Any HuC-3 game
  • Any weird GB/GBA/DS peripherals
  • Used Nintendo Switch consoles (preferably non-iPatched ones)
  • Used Macbook computers (from 2015 or newer)
